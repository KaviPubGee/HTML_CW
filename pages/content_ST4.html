Solutions That Work

Marrine Protected Areas

Establishing and expanding no-take zones allows marine ecosystems to recover and thrive. These areas serve as nurseries for fish populations and preserve biodiversity.

Climate Action

Addressing ocean acidification and warming through carbon dioxide removal technologies and reducing greenhouse gas emissions globally.

Sustainable Fishing

Implementing science-based fishing quotas and supporting coastal communities in adopting sustainable practices that protect marine life.

Plastic Pollution Reduction

Eliminationg single-use plastics and supporting cleanup initiatives to remove existing pollution from our oceans.

Costal Restoration

Restoring coral reefs, seagreass beds, and mangrove forests that serve as critical habitats and natural coastal protection.

Scientific Research

Supporting marine research and monitoring programs that guide conservation effort and policy decisions.
